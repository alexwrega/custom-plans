MobyMax Remediation Plan Summary

Focused 4-part plan: 42 highest-priority MobyMax Grade 4 lessons capped at 225 XP. Vocabulary and Inference come first because they underpin comprehension of every other skill type; Author’s Purpose (the largest single gap) is tackled in Part 2 alongside Text Features; later parts consolidate theme, summary, structure, and genre.

Part 1: Vocabulary & Inference
10 lessons 58 XP
  • Use definitions and examples to determine word meaning
  • Use compare-and-contrast context to unlock unknown words
  • Make inferences from details
  • Distinguish explicit from implicit information
  • Use details to support conclusions
Part 2: Author’s Purpose, Craft & Text Features
11 lessons 52 XP
  • Determine an author’s purpose and analyze text purpose
  • Recognize and explain anecdotes
  • First-person vs. third-person point of view
  • Analyze text features: headings, photos, charts, diagrams, graphs, timelines
Part 3: Theme, Main Idea, Summary & Text Structure
10 lessons 59 XP
  • Determine main topic and main idea of paragraphs
  • Summarize informational and literary texts
  • Identify theme in stories, dramas, and poems
  • Recognize chronological, compare-and-contrast, and other text structures
Part 4: Drama, Plot, Genre & Cross-Text
11 lessons 56 XP
  • Drama: scripts, stage directions, dialogue vs. stage directions
  • Plot structure and key story events
  • Firsthand vs. secondhand accounts
  • Compare and contrast myths and folk tales
  • Identify claims in persuasive texts
