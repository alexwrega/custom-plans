Skill Gap Analysis

Ranked by frequency of errors across 7 Grade 5 STAAR reading assessments (avg score 79.8%, 56 total wrong answers). Inference is by far the most persistent gap, followed by craft analysis and cross-text synthesis.

  • 1
    Inference & Drawing Conclusions
    Misses what a paragraph “suggests” or what the reader can “conclude” about a character’s feelings, motives, or situation — in stories, poems, dramas, and informational text alike.
    18 errors
  • 2
    Cross-Text Comparison & Synthesis
    Trouble with paired-text questions: shared messages, key differences in tone or focus, and how two authors approach the same topic.
    6 errors
  • 3
    Author’s Purpose & Craft
    Misses why an author uses figurative language, italics, capitalisation, rhetorical questions, or compare-and-contrast organisation in a passage.
    6 errors
  • 4
    Theme, Main Idea & Central Message
    Difficulty pinning down what an author is “emphasising” across a whole selection, the main message of a poem, or the central idea supported throughout an article.
    5 errors
  • 5
    Summary
    Picks summaries that include a partial detail or wrong emphasis instead of the choice that captures the whole story or section accurately.
    5 errors
  • 6
    Plot, Drama & Poetry Structure
    Confuses where resolutions begin, how stage directions reveal character, and how stanzas/lines support a poem’s theme. Includes point-of-view items.
    5 errors
  • 7
    Text Features & Graphics
    Mistakes on what a photograph illustrates, what a bar graph or diagram conveys, and which caption or text feature points to a specific detail.
    4 errors
  • 8
    Text Structure
    Misses how an author organises information — compare-and-contrast, chronological order, cause-and-effect — to help the reader understand a key idea.
    3 errors
  • 9
    Detail Retrieval & Text Evidence
    Picks weak evidence when asked which sentence best supports a claim or character trait.
    3 errors
  • 10
    Vocabulary & Context Clues
    Occasional misses on word meaning from context (withholding, agile). Generally a strength — but worth a quick refresh.
    2 errors
